Daily Meal Schedule 🍽

Follow this schedule for a balanced and healthy diet:

  • 🍳 Breakfast (7:00 AM - 9:00 AM): Start your day with a bowl of oatmeal, fresh fruits, and a glass of almond milk.
  • 🥜 Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM - 11:30 AM): Have a handful of nuts (almonds or walnuts) and a cup of green tea.
  • 🥗 Lunch (12:30 PM - 2:00 PM): Begin with a fresh salad 🥗, followed by multigrain roti, brown rice, and a serving of tofu or grilled chicken.
  • 🍓 Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM - 5:00 PM): Enjoy a fruit smoothie or a bowl of yogurt with chia seeds.
  • 🍲 Dinner (7:00 PM - 8:30 PM): Start with soup 🍲, followed by steamed vegetables, sweet potato, and a light protein like fish or lentils.
  • 🌙 Post-Dinner (Optional, 9:00 PM): A glass of warm almond milk with a pinch of turmeric for better sleep.

Tip: Always start your meal with lighter items like salads or soups to aid digestion and control portion sizes. 🥗
